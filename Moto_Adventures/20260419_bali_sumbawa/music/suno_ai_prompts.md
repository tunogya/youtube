# 🎵 YouTube 视频配乐：Suno AI 原创音乐伴奏指南 (Bali to Sumbawa)

为了避免 YouTube 的版权问题，我们将使用 Suno AI 来生成完全原创的纯音乐伴奏（Instrumental）。本指南提供了基于视频三个核心情绪章节的 AI Prompt，同时附带了在后期剪辑时推荐叠加的环境音效（SFX）思路。

> **Suno AI 提示技巧**：
> - 确保在 Suno 中勾选 **"Instrumental" (纯音乐)** 选项，不需要生成人声和歌词。
> - 生成后可以挑选合适的段落进行循环或裁剪。

---

## 🎸 Act 1: 启程与跨界 (Bali to Lombok)
**情绪与目标**：充满希望，带有公路旅行的自由感，逃离都市拥堵的释放。

**Suno AI Prompt:**
```text
[Instrumental]
Style tag: Cinematic Indie Folk, Modern Synthwave, Upbeat Acoustic Guitar, Road Trip Vibe, Hopeful, Driving Rhythm
Description: A driving and hopeful instrumental track. Starts with rhythmic acoustic guitar strumming and builds up with modern warm synthwave pads. Steady percussion that gives a feeling of forward momentum, freedom, and leaving the crowded city behind. Uplifting, energetic, but not too aggressive.
```

**后期音效叠加 (SFX Ideas):**
- 城市拥堵环境音快速渐弱：巴厘岛 Canggu 嘈杂的踏板车喇叭声。
- Kawasaki W175 的单缸引擎轰鸣声（启动、加速换挡的清脆声）。
- 呼啸而过的海风声（渡轮上的场景）。

---

## 🏜️ Act 2: 狂野与孤寂 (Entering Sumbawa)
**情绪与目标**：空间感，荒芜，孤寂，上帝视角下的渺小与震撼。

**Suno AI Prompt:**
```text
[Instrumental]
Style tag: Dark Country, Post-Rock, Ambient Drone, Heavy Reverb Slide Guitar, Vast, Empty, Deserted, Cinematic
Description: A vast, slow-building atmospheric track. Features sparse and haunting slide guitar with heavy reverb over deep ambient drone sounds. It feels like soaring over an endless, empty alien landscape. Minimal percussion, very wide soundscape, evoking a sense of isolation, awe, and the harsh beauty of a deserted wasteland.
```

**后期音效叠加 (SFX Ideas):**
- 船只引擎沉闷的底噪与低频共振（滚装船靠岸）。
- 干燥的狂风声与扬沙的声音。
- 只有偶尔几声远处的马嘶声（增添荒野感）。
- 轮胎碾压碎石路面的嘎吱声（越野大脚胎的摩擦声）。

---

## 🌋 Act 3: 高潮与沉淀 (Tambora & Lakey Peak)
**情绪与目标**：史诗感与极简的骤然对比，历史的厚重与自然的狂暴，最终归于平静。

**Suno AI Prompt (第一段：史诗高潮 - 适合航拍及海浪):**
```text
[Instrumental]
Style tag: Epic Orchestral, Tribal Percussion, Cinematic Tension, Dramatic, Huge Scale, Triumphant
Description: A massive, dramatic orchestral instrumental with powerful tribal percussion. Slowly builds tension into an epic, triumphant climax. Huge brass and sweeping strings mixed with local rhythmic drum beats. It sounds like facing a roaring force of nature or a giant volcano.
```

**Suno AI Prompt (第二段：极简沉淀 - 适合黑白人像与落日):**
```text
[Instrumental]
Style tag: Minimalist Piano, Ambient, Whisper-quiet, Peaceful, Emotional, Cinematic, Lo-Fi
Description: An extremely minimalist and quiet instrumental fade-out. Just a few slow, melancholic yet peaceful piano chords with a soft lo-fi ambient texture. It feels like the calm after a storm, fading into nothingness and reflection. Very spacious and raw.
```

**后期音效叠加 (SFX Ideas):**
- **高潮时**：沉闷而巨大的自然轰隆声（模拟火山的历史脉动，或是巨浪拍击礁石的爆炸声，注意避开高频刺耳音）。
- **沉淀时**：完全弱化音乐，仅保留极其纯净的环境白噪音（轻柔的海浪声或夜间微风的沙沙声）。
- 徕卡 M11-P 机械快门的清脆“咔嚓”声（作为转场和情绪定格的关键音效）。
- 两瓶 Bintang 啤酒玻璃瓶的清脆碰撞声（结尾淡出）。
