# 📸 YouTube 视频封面：AI 绘图 Prompt 指南 (Bali to Sumbawa)

为了制作极具吸引力的 YouTube 留存率封面（Thumbnail），以下为您归纳了适用于 Midjourney (v6)、DALL-E 3 等 AI 绘图工具的提示词。

> **💡 封面设计建议（Thumbnail Best Practices）：**
> - **留白**：构图时为 YouTube 右下角的时间戳、以及大号粗体文字（如 "ESCAPE BALI", "W175 TO SUMBAWA"）留出空间。
> - **对比度与饱和度**：户外探险类封面通常需要高对比度、清晰的主体轮廓、以及具有视觉冲击力的色彩（例如：强烈的夕阳逆光、荒漠的暖黄色与天空那一抹深蓝的对比）。
> - **长宽比**：YouTube 标准封面比例是 16:9，如果在 Midjourney 中生成，记得加上 `--ar 16:9` 参数。

---

## 🏜️ 概念一：荒野与复古机车的极致对比 (The Wasteland & The Classic Bike)
**核心情绪**：辽阔感、孤寂感、男人的浪漫

**Midjourney Prompt:**
```text
A cinematic, wide-angle shot of a dusty, customized vintage Kawasaki W175 motorcycle with off-road tires, parked on an empty dirt road in a vast, dry savannah landscape at golden hour. In the far background, a looming prehistoric volcano under a dramatic, cloudy sky. Extreme detail, National Geographic photography style, high contrast, warm golden lighting, dramatic sunset, negative space on the right side for text, shot on Leica M11, 35mm lens, 8k resolution --ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.0
```

## 🏍️ 概念二：第一人称的骑行冲击力 (First-Person Perspective Action)
**核心情绪**：代入感、极限冒险、速度感

**Midjourney Prompt:**
```text
First-person POV from the rider's seat of a vintage retro motorcycle, hands gripping leather handlebars. Speeding along a scenic coastal cliff road overlooking massive ocean waves. The rider is wearing rustic brown leather gloves. Vibrant blue ocean contrasting with dry yellow cliff grass. Extreme action shot, dynamic motion blur on the edges, highly immersive, GoPro Hero 12 style, hyper-realistic, dramatic lighting, empty sky perfect for thumbnail text --ar 16:9 --v 6.0
```


## 🌋 概念三：火山迷雾中的探险者 (The Explorer in the Volcanic Fog)
**核心情绪**：史诗感、未知、神秘

**Midjourney Prompt:**
```text
A wide epic shot of a solitary motorcyclist wearing an adventure helmet and rugged gear, riding a black scrambler motorcycle towards a massive, ash-covered mountain shrouded in dark, atmospheric fog. Somber and cinematic mood, harsh shadows, muted color palette with a striking contrast, cinematic lighting, Ridley Scott movie style, very detailed, rule of thirds, large negative space at the top --ar 16:9 --v 6.0
```

## 🗺️ 概念四：跨越海峡的背影 (Looking Across the Strait)
**核心情绪**：逃离感、远大前程

**Midjourney Prompt:**
```text
A cinematic shot from behind of a rider standing next to a fully loaded retro scrambler motorcycle at a dusty harbor, looking out at a rusty ferry boat on the ocean. The mood is adventurous and freeing. High resolution, shallow depth of field, sharp focus on the motorcycle and rider, cinematic color grading, vibrant but natural tones, leaving space on the left for bold typography --ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.0
```
